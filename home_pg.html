Welcome to Our Budget System

Submit your bills, manage expenses, and streamline approvals.

How to Manage Your Budget:

1. **Track Expenses**: Keep a record of all expenses, no matter how small. Use our system to submit bills promptly.

2. **Set Budget Goals**: Define spending limits for different categories (e.g., travel, supplies).

3. **Prioritize**: Allocate funds to essential needs first (e.g., utilities, salaries).

4. **Review Regularly**: Check your budget periodically to ensure you're on track.

5. **Collaborate**: Managers, review submitted bills promptly and approve or reject as needed.
